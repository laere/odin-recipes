Lasanga

Description

A classic italian favorite

Ingredients

  • Lasanga noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Drain the noodles
  3. Layer the noodles with the tomato sauce and ricotta cheese
  4. top with a generous serving of Mozzarella
  5. bake for 40 minutes
